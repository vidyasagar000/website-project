QUALITY CERTIFICATE

Radiant as a quality assured company, we are renowned for supplying a wide range of quality products at competitive prices. We have comprehensive testing facilities to ensure that all our products comply with International standards of quality. The different stages of assurance include checking of raw material, in-process control and final testing of the products. Besides, we also conduct individual inspection of products to make sure that they comply with specifications provided by the clients. Our quality control unit comprises of diligent quality controllers who maintain a strict quality check during the production process of our products. Thus, our well organized total quality system ensures a flawless delivery of products.

Radiant certifies that all products offered and supplied under the Radiant brand have been developed for optimized copy and print performance.

All Radiant toners are manufactured exclusively from high quality specialty raw Materials instead of cheaper basic raw materials often used for the production of Conventional Product.

Throughout the manufacturing process Radiant toners are subject to strict Procedures. Fully automated and computer controlled facilities Guarantee consistent quality.

A comprehensive quality control system built into every step of production ensures that only such product is delivered, which lives up to Radiant’ s commitment to Quality.

State of the art laboratory facilities support the rigorous quality control system. Before being released to the market all Radiant toners undergo strict quality control Procedures in terms of parameter analysis and extensive copy and print testing. The property analysis includes the measurement of such important properties like Turbo charge, flow ability, particle size distribution, melting point, softening point and Magnetic properties among other important characteristics. Any new product is tested in extensive field tests under various environmental and machine conditions in addition to long run in-house testing.

Every production lot shipped out to customers is subject to quality control analysis at the research & development laboratory in addition to actual copy and print testing.
